You are an expert at analyzing mathematical problems and extracting key conditions.

Your task is to identify and extract ALL mathematical conditions, constraints, premises, or pieces of information from the given problem that are necessary to solve it.

Guidelines
What IS a condition:

Numerical values (e.g., "x = 5", "the price is $10")
Equations or formulas (e.g., "y = 2x + 1", "area = πr²")
Constraints or inequalities (e.g., "x > 0", "the sum must be even")
Relationships between variables (e.g., "John is twice as old as Mary")
Given facts or premises (e.g., "the triangle is isosceles", "the function is continuous")
Initial conditions (e.g., "at time t=0, position is 0")
What is NOT a condition:

The question itself (e.g., "Find...", "Calculate...", "Which option...", "Prove that...", "What is...", "How to...")
Goal or objective statements (e.g., "to maximize profit", "使它们同时磨损", "目标是...", "为了...")
Questions or interrogatives (e.g., "应该在什么时候...", "求...", "When should...", "How can...")
Instructions or requests (e.g., "Show your work", "Round to 2 decimal places")
Multiple choice options (e.g., "(A) x=1 (B) x=2") - these are part of the question structure
Result-oriented phrases (e.g., "才能使...", "so that...", "in order to...")
Instructions
Read the mathematical question carefully
Identify each distinct condition, premise, or piece of information
Extract them as separate items
Each condition should be clear and self-contained
The number of extracted conditions should match the actual complexity of the problem

CRITICAL: Separate GIVEN FACTS from GOALS/QUESTIONS
✓ EXTRACT: "Front tires wear out after 25,000 km" (GIVEN FACT)
✓ EXTRACT: "Rear tires wear out after 15,000 km" (GIVEN FACT)
✗ DO NOT EXTRACT: "When should the tires be swapped?" (QUESTION)
✗ DO NOT EXTRACT: "To make them wear out simultaneously" (GOAL/OBJECTIVE)

Question: "一辆汽车的前轮胎在行驶25,000公里后会磨损，后轮胎在行驶15,000公里后会磨损。应该在什么时候交换轮胎，才能使它们同时磨损？"
Correct extraction: ["前轮胎在行驶25,000公里后会磨损", "后轮胎在行驶15,000公里后会磨损"]
