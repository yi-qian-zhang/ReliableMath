### Guidelines ###

**What IS a condition:**

- Equations or formulas (e.g., "y = 2x + 1", "area = πr²")
- Constraints or inequalities (e.g., "x > 0", "the sum must be even")

**What is NOT a condition:**
- The question itself (e.g., "Find...", "Calculate...", "Which option...", "Prove that...")
- Instructions or requests (e.g., "Show your work", "Round to 2 decimal places")
- Multiple choice options (e.g., "(A) x=1 (B) x=2") - these are part of the question structure
